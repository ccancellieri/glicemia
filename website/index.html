Why GliceMia?

Managing Type 1 Diabetes is a 24/7 job. Between carb counting, bolus calculations, activity adjustments, and trend watching, decision fatigue is real. Commercial solutions are expensive, closed-source, and often lack the personalized intelligence you need.

GliceMia is different

  • Runs on your own device — your data stays yours
  • Works with free AI models (Gemini, Ollama)
  • Connects to your Medtronic pump in real-time
  • Actually gives you numbers, not just "consult your doctor"
  • Open source — inspect, modify, contribute

How It Works

GliceMia runs on your own machine and communicates through Telegram. Here's the flow:

1

Connect Your Pump

Enter your CareLink credentials. GliceMia polls your Medtronic MiniMed 780G every 5 minutes for real-time glucose, IOB, reservoir level, and pump status.

2

Chat on Telegram

Open your Telegram bot. Ask questions in text or voice, send food photos, request your status, plan activities, or import health data — all through a friendly menu-driven interface.

3

Get Intelligent Answers

The AI analyzes your question with 12 layers of personal context. It provides concrete numbers: bolus suggestions with predicted glucose values, activity impact forecasts, and pattern insights.

4

Receive Proactive Alerts

GliceMia watches your glucose 24/7. When it detects a dangerous trend, predicts a low, or notices a pump issue, it alerts you immediately with context and suggested actions.

AI That Actually Helps

Unlike generic health chatbots, GliceMia's AI is designed to give you real, actionable answers.

Always Gives Numbers

GliceMia never says "consult your doctor" or "check with your Bolus Wizard." It always provides its own estimated bolus dose and predicted final glucose value, clearly labeled as educational suggestions.

Deeply Contextualized

Every response considers your current glucose, trend, IOB, recent meals, activity history, weekly patterns, monthly metrics, diagnosed conditions, and insulin settings. No two users get the same answer.

Predicts Forward

Instead of just telling you where you are, GliceMia tells you where you're going. "Your glucose is 180 and rising. Based on your IOB and trend, you'll likely be at 220 in 30 minutes."

I'm about to eat a plate of pasta with tomato sauce. Current glucose 145, trend stable.
GliceMia: I estimate ~65g of carbs for a standard plate of pasta with tomato sauce. With your current I:C ratio of 1:8 and ISF of 40, considering your IOB of 0.3U and glucose at 145 mg/dL:

Suggested bolus: 7.8U
Predicted glucose in 2h: ~130 mg/dL

This should bring you nicely into range. If it's a larger portion, add 1U per extra 8g of carbs.

Built on Solid Foundations

Enterprise-grade technology, completely open source.

Flexible AI

LiteLLM routes to any AI provider: free Gemini, local Ollama, Claude, GPT-4o, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint. Switch models with a single environment variable.

FHIR-Compliant Database

14 SQLAlchemy tables following FHIR health data standards. SNOMED-coded conditions, structured observations, and full insulin history. Optional AES-256 encryption with SQLCipher.

Real-Time Pipeline

Async Python with aiohttp and APScheduler. CareLink polling, alert detection, pattern computation, and Telegram message handling all run concurrently without blocking.

Health Integrations

CareLink real-time API, Apple Health ZIP import, FHIR client, lab PDF extraction. OpenRouteService for GPS routes, OpenWeatherMap for activity weather. All optional.

Python 3.11+ SQLAlchemy LiteLLM python-telegram-bot aiohttp matplotlib edge-tts SQLCipher

11 Intelligent Alert Types

GliceMia monitors your glucose 24/7 and alerts you before problems become emergencies.

URGENT
  • Hypoglycemia (<54 mg/dL)
  • Rapidly falling (>2.5 mg/dL/min)
  • Sensor data gap
  • Pump suspended
WARNING
  • Low glucose (70 mg/dL)
  • High glucose (250 mg/dL)
  • Predicted low/high
  • Rising fast (>3 mg/dL/min)
INFO
  • Reservoir running low
  • Battery running low
  • Prolonged high (>3h at >250)

Your Data, Your Control

GliceMia is designed with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought.

🏠

Runs Locally

GliceMia runs on your own device. Your health data never passes through our servers because there are no servers — it's your instance, your database.

🔒

Encrypted Storage

Optional AES-256 encryption via SQLCipher. Set a passphrase and your entire database is encrypted at rest. WAL mode ensures no unencrypted data touches disk.

👤

Access Control

Only allowed Telegram user IDs can interact with the bot. No public access, no shared accounts, no anonymous queries. Your data is accessible only to you.

📖

Open Source

Every line of code is auditable. AGPL-3.0 license means anyone can inspect how your data is handled. No hidden telemetry, no tracking, no data collection.

Get Started in Minutes

Three steps to your personal diabetes companion.

1

Clone & Configure

git clone https://github.com/ccancellieri/glicemia
cd glicemia
cp .env.example .env
# Edit .env with your keys
2

Install & Setup

python -m venv venv
source venv/bin/activate
pip install -r requirements.txt
python scripts/seed_demo.py
3

Run

python agent.py

Open Telegram and start chatting with your bot!

You need a Telegram Bot Token (free from @BotFather) and a Gemini API Key (free tier available). See the README for detailed setup instructions.
